Newsletter Editor’s Notes


Well, the United States of America has survived another Presidential Election. This time just barely. I am now thrilled to go to my mailbox and find much less than the two pounds of political flyers I had grown accustomed to lately. Whether you were for or against the winner and president-elect, I can’t think of a better way to celebrate or commiserate than by enjoying all the fun that skiing and the apre ski scene offer. And just remember that someone very important once reminded us that “seldom are your greatest fears or your greatest expectations realized.” OK, that’s as much editorial political philosophizing you are going to get from me.

Be sure to read Bill Melton’s page 10 report on the November 10th showing of the new Warren Miller film—Here, There and Everywhere. With the amazing attendance and opportunities to win free stuff, it was a huge success. Bill is the local producer for Warren Miller films. I bet it won’t be long before we see Bill starring in Miller’s films sporting his secret Glen Plake hair style.

Even though most of our ski trips are sold out, if you are a new member you might want to look at our website to see the details on just how good a deal each of the trips offer. The summary is on page 20 of the this newsletter just for reference. The Beaver Creek and the Greek Isles trips are still open, so get on board!
